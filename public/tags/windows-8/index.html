A Month with Windows 8

I got my hands on a copy of Windows 8 through MSDN about a month ago and installed it immediately. I’ve used it since then as my daily working machine on my main laptop. First of all the speed of Windows 8 is apparent from the start. Everything feels faster - boot time, application launch, etc. I am working on a 3 year old Lenovo W500 and I can honestly say that Windows 8 has extended its life by at least 6 months.
